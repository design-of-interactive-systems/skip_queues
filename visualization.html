Visual design

Designing the colors, fonts, images, shapes… requires a low fidelity prototype like mock-ups which define the visual feeling of the app, focusing the design on Android systems and testing it with experts.

Goals

• Decide the color palette of the application

• Decide the icons of the menus

• Select the images

• Decide the style of the components of the application

Steps

  • Step 1

    Colors

    The application needed a color palette that could identify the product and provide an attractive design to the system. The red was chosen as the dominant color since it transmits energy, dynamism and excitement; properties that should be related to the application.

  • Step 2

    Icons

    The system is designed for Android thus the icons followed the standards for this operating system. The selection of the icons was done trying to simplify the understanding of the user, because of that the relations between the icon and the related action must be straight forward: the menu uses a “burger menu” icon, the cart uses a cart icon and so on.

  • Step 3

    Images

    The application is based on images that represent the different kind of products offered, once a category is selected the actual products are presented and it is crucial that these images are eye catching, informative and simply beautiful. Users should feel as if they were glancing over real food.

  • Mock ups

    Step 4

    General style

    Finally, in order to enclose this section one mock-up per screen was done. These low fidelity prototypes contain the colors, images and icons selected in the previous steps and also the style of every component in the app including menus, buttons, cards, tabs…
